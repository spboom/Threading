What are gift subscriptions?

Gift subscriptions are a way for you to give the gift of NYTimes digital access. These are for 12 or 26 weeks, and your one-time payment covers the full subscription period. If gift recipients choose to extend access beyond the gift subscription period, they will enter their own billing information and pay for continued service.

Please note, gift subscriptions are for new digital subscribers only, the recipient cannot have an existing digital or home delivery subscription.

Can I purchase a gift subscription for someone who is already a subscriber?

No. You can only give a gift subscription to a new digital subscriber. If the intended recipient is an existing digital or home delivery subscriber, they won’t be able to redeem the gift.

How is the gift subscription delivered?

You will be required to supply a recipient e-mail address and delivery date during the purchase. An e-mail containing a redemption code is sent to the recipient e-mail address you provide on the date you specify. You will also have the option to include a short message that will be in the redemption e-mail.

If you choose to have the gift delivered immediately, your gift will be sent as soon as processing is complete. Processing of a gift subscription purchase normally takes 24 hours and may take up to 72 hours.

Can I purchase a home delivery gift subscription?

Yes. You can give a home delivery gift subscription by visiting here.

Can I purchase multiple gift subscriptions?

Yes. After completing a gift purchase, press the Buy Another Gift button. Please note, a single recipient cannot redeem multiple gift subscriptions at once.

You can also purchase multiple digital gift subscriptions or cards for your company or clients. Please e-mail us at corpgiftcard@nytimes.com or click here for more information.

Can I purchase gift subscriptions for my company or clients?

Yes. Please e-mail us at corpgiftcard@nytimes.com or click here for more information on The New York Times Corporate Gift Card Program.

Can I schedule the delivery of my gift?

Yes — you can set the date the gift e-mail will be sent to the recipient during the order process. The gift e-mail will not be sent until processing is complete. Processing of a gift subscription purchase normally takes 24 hours and may take up to 72 hours.

What are the terms of sale for gift subscriptions?

The full purchase price for gift subscriptions will be charged at the time of purchase and is nonrefundable.

Gift subscriptions are discussed under one-time purchases in the NYTimes Terms of Sale. Redemption codes are not replaceable and will expire if not redeemed within five years of the purchase date.
